French Onion Soup

Description

It's soup. Made with onions. It's pretty good, I think.

Ingredients

  • Onions, obviously, you should probably peel and cut them first
  • Beef stock, ideally make your own, recipe not provided
  • Wine
  • Gruyere, or whatever cheese you got
  • Croutons, or you could make your own if you feel like it, recipe not provided
  • Salt
  • Whatever other herbs or spices you feel like putting in

Steps

  1. Caramelize onions. Go slow. Don't burn them.
  2. Did you manage not to burn them? If so, good job, feel free to drink the wine as a reward. It's not actually used in this recipe. If you did burn them, start over and feel free to drink the wine to deal with your failure.
  3. Add beef stock.
  4. Simmer it for a while. It's soup, it's hard to mess up.
  5. Add salt to taste. I'm not your boss, add as much as you want.
  6. Pour it into some crocks or ramekins or something if you're fancy, or whatever kind of bowls you can safely put in the oven.
  7. Add croutons and cheese on top, put it under the broiler.
  8. Don't burn yourself eating it.
